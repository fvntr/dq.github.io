Integrated capital and software solutions for movement impact

Distributed Quorum provides expert, tech-driven guidance to movement organizations, offering one-on-one support to democratize capital and empower those advancing solutions for equitable and sustainable communities. We partner with established and emerging Community Capital Infrastructure (CCI) models—from grassroots foundations to community-controlled credit unions—helping movements build the capacity to absorb capital effectively and drive transformative change.

With an approach rooted in integrated capital and software-driven solutions, Distributed Quorum facilitates access to transformative funding, fostering resilient, community-owned systems. Our blend of strategic financial insight and tailored technology equips organizations to accelerate a just and inclusive economic future.
